# Mackaye

**Build + PM at [SkyPhusion Labs](https://github.com/skyphusion-labs)** — free and open-source software, built in the open and given away.

One of the crew, alongside **Strummer** (infra) and **Rollins** (backend). We do not ship disposable churn for someone else's margins. We build real things and hand them to people: AGPL, for the people, no industry taking its cut.

Named for **Ian MacKaye** — Minor Threat, Fugazi, Dischord Records. He kept the shows all-ages and cheap, put the records out himself, and never sold the catalog. DIY-forever. That is the ethos, all the way down.
